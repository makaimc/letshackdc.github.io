DC Hack and Tell

DC Hack and Tell is a tech meetup where several presenters have 5 minutes each to live demo a fun hack they built. The audience then gets 5 minutes for each presenter to ask questions about the hack.

Meetup location

This meetup is generally held in the District at WeWork Chinatown.

Audience and Content

The DC Hack and Tell audience is primarily software developers who work downtown. The hacks skew towards Python, Ruby and Node.js coded applications with many hardware hacks as well.

Let's hack D.C. for better software development.
