## Summary
[TrackMaven](http://trackmaven.com/) is a competitive marketing intelligence 
platform.


## Office Location
Just south of Dupont Circle. Accessible by red, orange and blue metro lines.


## Software stack
* AngularJS
* Python, Django, django-rest-framework
* Amazon Web Services
