About

There has been a surge of interest in tech startups during the past several years in the District. However, tech startups != software development, and we care about great software development.

Great software development comes about by engaged developers with the freedom to creatively solve problems using technology. Engaged developers attend events with their peers that get them excited about new programming techniques, languages and frameworks. These events should not be interfered with by recruiters, "idea guys" and contracting firms looking to make a quick buck off developers working diligently to improve their craft.
